# Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1 — Title

**LingoBites**  
Turn real-world English into structured Vietnamese lessons.

Include:

- App name
- One-line value proposition
- Founder/contact

---

## Slide 2 — Problem

Vietnamese beginners encounter English in daily life but do not know how to learn from it.

Pain points:

- Translation apps only give meaning, not how to use the language.
- Learners do not know sentence structure, grammar, or which words matter.
- Real-world content is ignored—not saved as a lesson.

---

## Slide 3 — Current Alternatives Are Incomplete

| Alternative | Gap |
|---|---|
| Google Translate/Lens | Fast translation but no lesson creation |
| Duolingo | Has curriculum but not personalized to real-world text |
| ELSA | Focused on speaking/pronunciation |
| ChatGPT | Powerful but lacks dedicated mobile flow: scan, confirm, structured lesson, save/review |

Message:

```text
The market has translation tools and learning apps, but not a focused learning-from-real-world-text loop for Vietnamese beginners.
```

---

## Slide 4 — Solution

Core loop:

```text
Scan/Input → Confirm Text → Generate Lesson → Learn → Save → Review
```

Show:

- Image/text input
- OCR review/edit
- Lesson result
- Save/history

---

## Slide 5 — Product Demo Flow

Use one concrete example:

```text
"We are offering a special discount for new customers."
```

Output:

- Vietnamese translation
- Sentence breakdown
- Vocabulary: offer, discount, customer
- Grammar: present continuous
- Pronunciation guide
- Quick practice

Goal: make investor understand the experience in 30 seconds.

---

## Slide 6 — Target User

Primary:

- Vietnamese beginners / weak foundations
- Need Vietnamese explanations
- Want practical English from daily life

Initial wedge:

- Students
- Office workers
- Self-learners
- Travelers

---

## Slide 7 — Why This Can Work Now

- OCR and mobile cameras are mainstream.
- AI can generate structured tutoring output.
- Users are comfortable learning with AI-assisted tools.
- BYOK + managed AI makes unit economics more controllable.

---

## Slide 8 — Business Model

| Tier | Value | Monetization |
|---|---|---|
| Free | Demo/trial or BYOK | Low system AI cost |
| Paid | Managed AI, no setup required | Subscription or credit bundle |
| Future premium | Review, flashcards, speaking, advanced import | Higher ARPU |

Key point:

```text
Free users can reduce system cost via BYOK; paid users pay for convenience.
```

---

## Slide 9 — Unit Economics and Cost Guard

Use operating estimates:

| Item | Estimate |
|---|---:|
| Paste text lesson | ~0.003-0.008 USD |
| Image lesson | ~0.005-0.015 USD |
| Closed beta small | ~30-100 USD/month |

Cost controls:

- 3,000 character limit
- no AI recall for saved lessons
- retry max 1
- quota/rate limit
- BYOK for free/power users

---

## Slide 10 — Roadmap

| Phase | Goal |
|---|---|
| Phase 0 | MVP scan/paste → lesson → save/review |
| Phase 1 | Flashcard, spaced repetition, daily review |
| Phase 2 | Speaking/shadowing, AI tutor Q&A |
| Phase 3 | PDF/web import, premium learning path, monetization |

## Slide 11 — Traction Plan

30/60/90-day validation:

| Period | Goal |
|---|---|
| 30 days | Internal beta, result quality, OCR sample set |
| 60 days | Closed beta, usage/retention signals |

Metrics:
